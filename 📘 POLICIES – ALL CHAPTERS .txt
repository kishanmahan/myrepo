📘 POLICIES – ALL CHAPTERS (END-TO-END)
🟢 CHAPTER 1: Policy Overview
Policy kya hoti hai?
👉 Rule jo check karta hai ki koi access conflict ya risk create to nahi kar raha.
Purpose:-
Compliance
Risk reduction
SoD enforcement
Audit readiness

🟢 CHAPTER 2: Types of Policies
Mainly 3 types:-
1️⃣ Segregation of Duties (SoD) Policy
2️⃣ Activity-Based Policy
3️⃣ Advanced Policy (Rule-based)

🟢 CHAPTER 3: Segregation of Duties (SoD)
Most common 🔥
Example:-
User cannot have:-
Create Vendor
Approve Payment
Conflict detect hoga if dono access present.

Components:-
Left side access
Right side access
Risk level
Violation owner

🟢 CHAPTER 4: Entitlement vs Role Based SoD
Policy create kar sakte ho:-
Entitlement vs Entitlement
Role vs Role
Role vs Entitlement
Flexible governance model.

🟢 CHAPTER 5: Policy Risk Scoring
Each policy me define hota hai:-
Risk score (numeric)
Severity (Low / Medium / High / Critical)
Violation hone pe:-
Identity risk score increase hota hai.

🟢 CHAPTER 6: Policy Violation Lifecycle
Flow:-
Access assign →
Policy engine evaluate →
Violation detect →
Risk update →
Certification trigger (optional) →
Remediation

🟢 CHAPTER 7: Policy Enforcement Points
Policy check hota hai:-
1️⃣ Access request time
2️⃣ Role assignment time
3️⃣ Identity refresh
4️⃣ Aggregation
5️⃣ Certification

🟢 CHAPTER 8: Preventive vs Detective Policy
Preventive:-
Request block karta hai

Detective:-
Allow karta hai but violation record karta hai

🟢 CHAPTER 9: Policy Violation Actions
Options:-
✔ Block provisioning
✔ Allow with approval
✔ Allow with mitigation
✔ Notify security

🟢 CHAPTER 10: Mitigation
If violation accepted:-
Business justification required
Expiry date define
Compensating control mention
Audit evidence store hota hai.

🟢 CHAPTER 11: Activity-Based Policies
Check karta hai:-
User activity logs
High-risk behavior
Less common but advanced governance me use hota hai.

🟢 CHAPTER 12: Advanced Rule-Based Policies
Custom logic use karte hain:-
BeanShell rule
Complex attribute comparison
Conditional checks
Example:-
If department = Finance
AND role = Admin
→ Violation

🟢 CHAPTER 13: Policy Scope
Define kar sakte ho:-
Specific application
Specific department
Specific identity type
High-risk users only

Best practice 🔥

🟢 CHAPTER 21: Performance Considerations

Thousands of identities
Multiple policies

Optimize:-

Use scoped policies

🟢 CHAPTER 22: False Positives Handling

Wrong mapping
Incomplete role design

Solution:-
Fine-tune policy
Add exclusions
Adjust scope
