3.<Arg name="enableRetryRequest" value="false"/>
👉 Ye setting SailPoint provisioning engine ko batati hai ki failed request ko automatically retry karna hai ya nahi.
Meaning:-
enableRetryRequest="false"
👉 Matlab:-
Agar provisioning request fail ho jaye, to system automatically dobara try nahi karega.
Ye SailPoint ke “LCM Provisioning” workflow (called workflow) ko bheje ja rahe hain.
