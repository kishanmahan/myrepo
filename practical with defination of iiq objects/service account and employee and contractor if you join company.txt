()Service Account ek "System" ya "Application" ka account hota hai.
1. Service Account ka Asli Kaam:-
--Service accounts ka use tab hota hai jab ek system ko dusre system se baat karni ho ya koi automation chalana ho.
--System-to-System Communication: Jaise SailPoint ko agar Active Directory (AD) se data lana hai, 
toh SailPoint ko AD mein login karne ke liye ek account chahiye hoga. Wo account "Service Account" kehlayega.
--Background Tasks: Ye accounts tasks, workflows, ya aggregations ko backend mein run karne ke liye use hote hain.
--Permissions: Inke paas specific permissions hoti hain(jaise Read/Write access) taaki ye bina kisi manual interference ke apna kaam kar sakein.

2. Service Account vs User Account:-
Feature	                                   User Account	                               Service Account
Owner:---------------------------------Ek zinda insaan (Employee):------------------Ek Application ya Service
Login:----------------Password aur MFA ka use karke login hota hai:------API keys, Certificates, ya non-expiring passwords ka use hota hai.
Password Policy:------Password periodic change hota rehta hai:-----------Aksar password expire nahi hota(varna integration break ho jayegi).
Activity:-------------Business tasks (Email, Apps use karna):------------Automation, Data Aggregation, Provisioning.

3. IIQ mein Service Account ka Use Case
Aapne jo abhi tak kaam kiya hai, usme Service Account yahan kaam aata hai:-

--AD Integration: Jab aapne Active Directory ko SailPoint se connect kiya tha, 
tab aapne ek username/password dala hoga (Domain Admin ya Service user).Wo ek Service Account hai.

--DB Connection: Jab SailPoint aapke MySQL/Oracle database se connect karta hai, 
toh wo ek service account ka use karke hi identities fetch karta hai.

--API Calls: Agar aap koi external website ya portal bana rahe hain jo SailPoint se data maang raha hai, 
toh wahan authentication ke liye Service Account use hoga.

4.Kuch Zaroori Baatein (Security Perspective):-
--Least Privilege: Service account ko sirf utni hi permission deni chahiye jitni kaam ke liye zaroori ho.
--No Interactive Login: In accounts ko kabhi bhi computer par manually login (RDP/Desktop) karne ki permission nahi deni chahiye.
--Governance: SailPoint mein hum Service Accounts ko bhi Certify karte hain taaki ye pata rahe ki ye account abhi bhi zaroori hai ya nahi.
