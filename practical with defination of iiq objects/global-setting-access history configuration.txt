1. Access History Configuration:-
Jab aap Enable Access History ko check karte hain, toh SailPoint user ke access badlavon ka ek timeline record karna shuru kar deta hai.

Maqsad (Purpose): Yeh dekhne ke liye ki kisi user ke paas purane samay mein kya access tha.

Kaise Kaam Karta Hai: Yeh identities aur unke assigned roles/entitlements ke snapshots leta hai.

Benefit: Agar audit mein pucha jaye ki "Pichle saal June mein is user ke paas kaunse accounts the?", 
toh Access History se uska jawab mil jayega. Yeh compliance ke liye bahut zaroori hai.
