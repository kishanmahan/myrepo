IIQ me ye time-based activation/deactivation concept hai.

1. Sunrise ☀️

Sunrise = kab access/role/entitlement start (active) hoga.

Matlab:

Effective From Date / Start Date

Example:

Aaj 22 May hai.

Tumne role assign kiya:

Role = HR_Access
Sunrise = 01 June 2026

Meaning:

01 June se pehle role inactive
01 June ko role automatically active

2. Sunset 🌙

Sunset = kab access/role/entitlement expire/inactive hoga.

Matlab:

End Date / Expiry Date

Example:

Role = Contractor_Access
Sunset = 30 June 2026

Meaning:

30 June ke baad access automatically remove/expire
