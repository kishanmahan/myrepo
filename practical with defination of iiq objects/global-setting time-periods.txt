()global-setting->time-periods->

()Configure Time Periods upyog system ke alag-alag kamo (jaise access requests, certifications, ya notifications)
ke liye samay tay karne ke liye kiya jata hai.

()Quarters (First, Second, Third, Fourth):-
Kya hai?: Yeh poore saal ko teen-teen mahino ke char hisso mein baantta hai.
Kaam: Inka upyog aksar Quarterly Access Reviews ke liye hota hai taaki har teen mahine mein employees ke access ko check kiya ja sake.

()Holidays:-
Kya hai?: chhuttiyon (jaise 1st Jan, 25th Dec) ki ek list.
Kaam: Agar koi onboarding ya offboarding task chhutti ke din pad raha hai, toh system ise detect karke use agle working day par shift kar sakta hai.

()Holidays:-
Kya hai?: Saalgiri ki chhuttiyon (jaise 1st Jan, 25th Dec) ki ek list.
Kaam: Agar koi onboarding ya offboarding task chhutti ke din pad raha hai, 
toh system ise detect karke use agle working day par shift kar sakta hai.

()Weekdays & Weekends:-
Kya hai?: Somvar se Shukravar (Weekdays) aur Shani-Ravi (Weekends).
Kaam: System policies banate waqt kaam aata hai—jaise "Emergency Access" sirf weekends par hi maanga ja sake.

()Inka Istemal Kahan Hota Hai?
IdentityIQ mein in periods ka istemal mukhya roop se teen jagahon par hota hai:-
Workgroups: Kaam distribute karne ke liye.
Certification Schedules: Periodic reviews set karne ke liye.
Policy Violations: Yeh check karne ke liye ki kya koi access sirf specific samay par hi allowed hai.
