()Ab Main Concept:-Optimistic Provisioning kya hota hai?
Normally IIQ ka flow:-
Provisioning request gaya
        ↓
Target system update hua
        ↓
Aggregation/refresh hua
        ↓
Identity cube update hua

Isme delay hota hai.
Example:-
User ko AD group diya:-
Finance_Group
Provisioning request bheja gaya.

Lekin:-
identity cube tab tak update nahi hota jab tak:
aggregation
refresh
na chale.

Optimistic Provisioning ON
Agar:-
optimisticProvisioning = true
toh:
Provisioning request gaya
        ↓
IIQ maan lega ki success ho gaya
        ↓
Immediately identity cube update kar dega.
