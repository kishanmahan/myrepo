()Schema mapping kya karta hai?
--Schema Mapping ka matlab hai application ke fields ko SailPoint ke fields se match karna.
Application aur SailPoint ke field names alag ho sakte hain.

Example:-
Application fields:-
uid
mail
department

SailPoint fields:-
name
email
dept

Mapping:-
uid → name
mail → email
department → dept

Isko schema mapping bolte hain.
