()Target System kya hai?
Target System = wo external application ya system jahan user ka account ya access manage hota hai.
Matlab SailPoint khud account store nahi karta.
Wo sirf identity governance tool hai jo dusre systems ko control karta hai.
Un systems ko Target Systems bolte hain.

Example:-
Company me employee Rahul hai.

()Uske accounts ho sakte hain:-
Active Directory
SAP
Jira
Salesforce
Email
Database
Ye sab Target Systems hain.
SailPoint in sab me access manage karta hai.
