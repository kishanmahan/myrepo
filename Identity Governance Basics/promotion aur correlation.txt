🔥 ✅ 1️⃣ Correlation kya hota hai?
👉Correlation = “Account kis Identity ka hai?” ye find karna

🧠 Simple example:-
External system (AD) me user aaya:-
username = john.doe
email = john@gmail.com

👉 Ab system poochta hai:-
"Ye account IdentityIQ me kis user ka hai?"

🔥 Matching ka process:-
System match karta hai:-
account.email == identity.email

👉 Agar match mil gaya:-
✔ Same identity se link ho jayega

👉 Agar match nahi mila:-
✔ New identity create ho sakta hai.
------------------------------------------------
🔥 ✅ 2️⃣ Promotion kya hota hai?
💡 ONE LINE
👉
Promotion = Account ke data ko Identity me copy/update karna

🧠 Simple example:-
Account me hai:-
email = john@gmail.com
department = IT

👉 Promotion karega:-
identity.email = john@gmail.com
identity.department = IT
