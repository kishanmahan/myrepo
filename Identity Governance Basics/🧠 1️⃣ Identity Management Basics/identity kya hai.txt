()Identity = Digital Person
Identity ka purpose: Ye system ko batata hai kaun kaun hai aur kya kya access hai.
Components of an Identity:-
Basic Info: Name, Email, Employee ID, Department
Credentials: Username, Password, MFA info
Roles & Groups: Admin, Manager, Finance, IT
Permissions: Kya kya system ya application me access hai
Status: Active, Inactive, Suspended
Example:-
Rahul ka Identity object me ye ho sakta hai:-
Name: Rahul Kumar
Email: rahul@company.com
Department: Finance
Role: Finance Manager
Applications Access: SAP, Salesforce
Status: Active

Ye Identity SailPoint me identity object ke roop me save hota hai.
SailPoint iske basis pe access request, provisioning, and certification manage karta hai.
aur identity me bahut sare users(employees,admin,staff,manager,owner) ho sakte hai.
aur har user ke bahut sare accounts alag alag applications ke ho sakte hai.
