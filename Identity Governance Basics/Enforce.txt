2️⃣ Enforce (लागू करना / लागू करना):-
Enforce ka matlab hota hai rules ya policies ko actively apply karna, taki koi bhi violation ya unauthorized access na ho.
Example:-
Policy: Finance team ke alawa koi write access nahi le sakta.
Enforce karne par SailPoint automatically access ko block ya remove kar dega jo policy violate kar raha hai.

()SailPoint me enforce methods:-
--Policy Enforcement – Automatically check aur remediate violations.
PolicyViolation object track karta hai violations.
ProvisioningPlan ya ProvisioningProject ke through action trigger hota hai.

--Manual enforcement – Admin manually entitlement revoke ya approve kar sakta hai.
