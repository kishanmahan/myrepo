()SailPoint mein Severity ka matlab hota hai ki koi Policy Violation kitna khatarnak ya "risky" hai.
Yeh ek ranking system hai jo security team ko batati hai ki unhe sabse pehle kis violation ko fix karna chahiye.

1. Severity Levels (Types):-
SailPoint mein standard 4 levels hote hain:-

()Critical(Highest):-
Kab use hota hai:-Jab koi aisa access mil jaye jisse company ko turant bada nuksan ho sakta ho 
(e.g., Direct Database Access ya Admin rights).
Impact: Iska violation hote hi user ka Risk Score drastically badh jata hai.

()High:-
Kab use hota hai: Serious SoD violations (e.g., ek hi person Paisa transfer bhi kar sakta hai aur approve bhi).
Impact: Inhe 24-48 hours ke andar resolve karna mandatory hota hai.

()Medium:-
Kab use hota hai: Aise violations jo policy ke khilaf hain par turant bada khatra nahi hain (e.g., Department mismatch).

()Low:-
Kab use hota hai: Minor compliance issues ya informational alerts.

2.Severity ka Risk Score par Asar:-
SailPoint mein Severity seedha Identity Risk Score se judi hoti hai.
Iska calculation kuch is tarah chalta hai:-

()Total RiskScore = (Base Score) + (Policy Violation Score * times Severity Weight)

()1. Base Score (The Foundation):-
Base Score user ki Identity Attributes aur Roles par depend karta hai, bina kisi violation ke.
Kya hai: Yeh wo risk hai jo user ke "Job Title" ya "Department" ke saath inherent hota hai.
Example: Ek "IT Admin" ka Base Score hamesha ek "Receptionist" se zyada hoga,
kyunki Admin ke paas sensitive systems ka access pehle se hai.
Calculation: SailPoint ke Risk Configuration mein define kiya jata hai ki kis application ya attribute ka kitna base weight hoga.

()2. Policy Violation Score (The Event):-
Yeh score tab trigger hota hai jab user koi rule todta hai (e.g., SoD violation).
Kya hai: Yeh wo "penalty points" hain jo user ke profile par lagte hain 
jab wo koi aisi combination of access le leta hai jo mana hai.
Logic: Agar user ne koi policy nahi todi, toh uska Policy Violation Score 0 rahega.
Jaise hi wo rule todta hai, yeh score active ho jata hai.

3. Severity Weight (The Multiplier):-
Yeh sabse crucial part hai jo violation ki "gammbheerta" (seriousness) batata hai.
Kya hai: Har Severity level (Critical, High, Medium, Low) ka ek numerical Weight hota hai.
Example Weights:-
Critical = 100
High = 75
Medium = 50
Low = 25

()Base Score:-150 (User Manager level par hai).
Policy Violation:-User ne SoD rule toda(Invoice Create + Approve).
Violation Score:-Maan lete hain 2 (Standard value).
Severity Weight:-Kyunki yeh financial fraud risk hai,
iski Severity "Critical" hai (Weight = 100).
Calculation:$150 + (2 \times 100) = \mathbf{350}$
