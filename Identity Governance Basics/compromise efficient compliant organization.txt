()1️⃣ Compromise kya hai (SailPoint context):-
Compromise ka matlab hack nahi hai.
Ye policy violation ke liye business exception allow karna hota hai.
Mostly use hota hai:-
SoD (Segregation of Duties) violations
Policy violations
Access risk

✅ Simple definition:-
Jab koi user ya account normally rule violate karta hai,
lekin business reason se exception approve kar di jati hai, usko compromise kehte hain.

2️⃣ Compromise kya karta hai?
Risk ko temporarily accept karta hai
User ko necessary role ya access diya jata hai, jo normally rule violate karta hai.
Audit aur reporting me track hota hai
Pata hota hai ki kaunse exception approve hue.
Business continuity maintain karta hai
Zaruri operations block nahi hote, lekin risk controlled rehta hai.

3️⃣ Compromise ke andar kya hota hai?
PolicyViolation object – Kaunse rule ya policy violate hui hai
Reason / Justification – Manager ya business ne exception kyu allow kiya
Status – Violation ka status “Compromised” ho jata hai.

4️⃣ Example (Java - IdentityIQ):-
PolicyViolation violation = context.getObjectByName(PolicyViolation.class, "SoD Conflict");

// Agar violation business critical hai to compromise allow karo
if(violation.isBusinessCritical()) {
    violation.compromise("Manager approved exception");
}

// Status check karo
System.out.println("Violation status: " + violation.getStatus());

Output:-
Violation status: Compromised

1️⃣ violation.compromise("Manager approved exception");:-
Kya karta hai:-
Ye policy violation ko compromise status me set karta hai.
Matlab, normally ye rule violate ho raha hai, lekin business reason / manager approval ke liye exception allow kar di ja rahi hai.

Parameter explanation:-
"Manager approved exception" → ye reason ya justification hai ki kyun violation compromise ho raha hai.

Effect:-
Violation object ka status change ho jata hai “Compromised”.
System me ye recorded hota hai ki exception allow ki gayi hai aur kaun approve kiya.

2️⃣ violation.getStatus();:-
Kya karta hai:-
Ye current status of violation ko return karta hai.
Example: "Active" (rule still violated), "Compromised" (exception approved), "Resolved" (issue fix ho gaya).

Effect:-
Ye read-only operation hai, kuch change nahi karta.
Hum status check karne ke liye use karte hain.
