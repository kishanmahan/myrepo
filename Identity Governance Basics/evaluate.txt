1️⃣ Evaluate ka matlab:-
Policy.evaluate(identity) → Ye check karta hai ki di hui identity (user) ke paas allowed access hai ya koi violation ho rahi hai.
Agar violation hoti hai → SailPoint me Policy Violation create hoti hai.

2️⃣ Kaise kaam karta hai:-
Identity ke current entitlements/roles/access lete hain.
Policy ke rules check karte hain:-
Example: Policy me likha hai “Finance role wale sirf Finance app access kar sakte hain.”
Identity ke access me agar koi non-Finance app access hai → Violation.

()Return karta hai result:-
Boolean (true/false) ya
Detailed violation object (kaunsa rule break hua, kaunsa access galat hai).
