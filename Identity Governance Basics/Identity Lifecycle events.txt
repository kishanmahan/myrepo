🔄 1️⃣6️⃣ Identity Lifecycle Events:-
--1️⃣ Identity Lifecycle kya hai/
Identity lifecycle ka matlab hai user account ka complete journey:
Joiner → Mover → Leaver
Yani:-
Joiner → naya employee join karta hai
Mover → employee ka department / role change hota hai
Leaver → employee company chhod deta hai

2️⃣ Important Identity Lifecycle Events:-
1️⃣ Identity Creation (Joiner Event)

Jab naya employee system me create hota hai.

Example:

HR System
   ↓
New Employee Record
   ↓
Identity Create in IAM
Output:-
User: Rahul
Department: IT
Access: Email + VPN

2️⃣ Identity Update (Mover Event):-
Jab employee ka role ya department change hota hai.

Example:-
Department change
IT → Finance

IAM kya karega:-
old access remove karega
new access assign karega
Output:-
Remove: IT Tools
Add: Finance Application

3️⃣ Identity Disable / Delete (Leaver Event):-
Jab employee company chhod deta hai.
System automatically:-
account disable karega
access revoke karega

Example:-
Employee resigned
   ↓
IAM disable accounts
   ↓
All access removed

()Rehire kya hai?
--👉 Rehire ka matlab hai jab koi employee company chhodne ke baad dobara same company me join karta hai.

1️⃣ Rehire kya hota hai?
Example:-
2023 → Rahul company join karta hai
2024 → Rahul resign kar deta hai
2025 → Rahul fir se company join karta hai
Is situation ko Rehire bolte hain.

2️⃣ IAM system Rehire me kya karta hai?
Jab rehire hota hai to IAM system:-
1️⃣ Old identity ko check karta hai
2️⃣ Agar identity exist karti hai to reactivate karta hai
3️⃣ Required access fir se assign karta hai

Flow:-
HR System
   ↓
Employee Rehire
   ↓
IAM detect rehire
   ↓
Identity Reactivate
   ↓
Access Provision
------------------------------------------------------
()Contractor lifecycle kya hai?
--Contractor Lifecycle IAM/SailPoint me contract employees (temporary workers)
ke identity aur access ka poora lifecycle management hota hai.
Simple words me:-
👉 Contractor jab company me temporary kaam ke liye aata hai,usko access dena, manage karna 
aur contract khatam hone par access hata dena — is process ko Contractor Lifecycle bolte hain.
