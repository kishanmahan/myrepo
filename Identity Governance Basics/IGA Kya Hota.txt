🛡️ IGA Kya Hota Hai?
IGA = Identity Governance and Administration
Ye ek security framework hai jo manage karta hai:
Kaun user hai 👤
Usko kya access milna chahiye 🔑
Kisne approve kiya ✔️
Kab revoke hua ❌
Audit proof kya hai 📊
Enterprise level pe use hota hai.

Example tools:-

SailPoint IdentityNow
Saviynt
Oracle Identity Governance
