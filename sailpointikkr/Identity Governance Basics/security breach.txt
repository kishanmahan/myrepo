1️⃣ Security Breach = सुरक्षा उल्लंघन / Unauthorized Access,
Matlab koi bina permission ke system ya data me ghus jaye.
Ye intentional (hacker) ya accidental (employee mistake) dono ho sakta hai.

2️⃣ Why it’s serious:-
Sensitive data leak ho sakta hai (financial info, health records, personal data).
Company ka trust aur legal compliance khatam ho sakta hai.
Laws like SOX, GDPR, HIPAA me security breach ke liye penalties aur reporting mandatory hoti hai.

3️⃣ Examples of Security Breach
Type	                             Example
Hacking:---------------Hacker company ke financial database me ghus ke records chura le.
Unauthorized Access:---Employee ne confidential data ko access kiya jo uske role me nahi tha.
Data Leak:-------------Customer emails ya medical records accidentally public ho gaye.
Malware/Ransomware:----Virus ya ransomware se system data inaccessible ho gaya.

4️⃣ Response to Security Breach:-
Detect: Logs aur monitoring se pata chale ki breach hua.
Contain: Breach ko immediately stop kare (system disconnect, access block).
Notify: Laws ke hisab se affected users ya authorities ko inform kare (GDPR me 72 hours, HIPAA me jaldi).
Recover: Backup se data restore kare, aur future prevention ke liye security strengthen kare.

Mandatory” ka matlab hai अनिवार्य / ज़रूरी.
