🔷 1️⃣ EscalationLevel
🔹 Kya hai:-
WorkItem me current escalation stage ko represent karta hai.
Jab approver action nahi leta, workflow escalate hota hai higher authority ya manager ko.
Ye level track karta hai ki abhi WorkItem original approver ke paas hai ya escalate ho chuka hai.
🔹 Possible Levels:-
Level	         Meaning
0	Original approver (first person)
1	First escalation (manager / next in line)
2	Second escalation (higher manager / admin)
3+	Further escalation steps (optional, workflow dependent)
