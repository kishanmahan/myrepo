🔹 ViolationOwner ka matlab:-
ViolationOwner = wo banda jo specific violation ka responsible hai
Ye banda violation ka resolution handle karta hai
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
🔹 Responsibilities of ViolationOwner:-
Policy ya violation create / edit / update kar sakta hai
Agar violation nayi identify hui hai, to wo create karega
Agar existing violation me changes chahiye, wo update karega
Review action le sakta hai
Apne aap review complete kar sakta hai ✅
Ya kisi aur reviewer ko notify kar sakta hai (delegate / assign)
Temporary Mitigation apply kar sakta hai
Agar immediate action nahi liya ja sakta, to mitigation set kar sakta hai
Example: "Manager Approval" ya "Temporary Access Justification"

🔹 Example Flow:-
User Rahul ke paas Admin role hai, par policy violation hai
ViolationOwner: Manager1

Step 1: Manager1 check karta hai → violation validate
Step 2: Manager1 action leta hai:
    - Direct revoke (permanent)
    - Approve temporary access (mitigation)
    - Notify aur assign dusre reviewer
Step 3: System updates violation status
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
🔹 AccessReviewOwner ka matlab:-
👉 Owner = wo Identity (user) jo policy, access review ya certification campaign ka responsible hai
Ye banda policy create karega, edit karega, ya review monitor karega
Basically “accountable person”
