()1️⃣ Potential Risk:-
Meaning: संभावित खतरा / कोई problem jo future me ho sakti hai.
Example:-
Agar company ka backup system weak hai → potential risk hai ki data loss ho sakta hai.

()2️⃣ Detect:-
Meaning: पता लगाना / पहचानना
Example:-
Security logs check karke pata lagana ki koi unauthorized access hua ya nahi.
Malware ya virus detect karna.

()3️⃣ Assess:-
Meaning: मूल्यांकन करना / Evaluate karna
Example:-
Risk ka impact aur probability check karna.
Example: Data loss hone ka potential loss kitna hoga, high ya low?

()4️⃣ Mitigate:-
Meaning: कम करना / Reduce karna
Example:-
Risk ko minimize karne ke liye action lena.
Example: Strong backup policy aur encryption implement karna → data loss ka risk mitigate ho jata hai.

()5️⃣ Identify Toxic Combinations:-
Meaning: ऐसे combination ko identify karna jo problem ya risk create kar sakte ho.
Example in IT / Compliance:-
Ek user ko financial approval + vendor payment dono access de diya → fraud ka risk → toxic combination hai.
SOX aur internal control me ye identify karna mandatory hai.

()6️⃣ Non-compliant:-
Meaning: नियमों का पालन न करने वाला / Rule ya law follow na karna
Example:-
GDPR me user consent ke bina personal data collect karna → non-compliant hai.
HIPAA me PHI encrypt na karna → non-compliant hai.
