()Reflect ka matlab hai – “dikhana” ya “prakat karna”.
Jab koi cheez reflect hoti hai, iska matlab hai ki uska effect ya result samne aa raha hai.
Example everyday life:-
“Mirror me apna chehra reflect hota hai” → mirror me dikh raha hai.
“Policy violation reflect ho gayi dashboard me” → dashboard me violation ka effect dikh raha hai.
