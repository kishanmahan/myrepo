()Compliance Standards / Regulations hain jo companies ko security, 
data protection aur auditing rules follow karne ke liye banaye gaye hain.
IAM tools jaise SailPoint IdentityIQ aur Okta inko follow karne me help karte hain.

1️⃣ ISO 27001:-
ISO 27001 = Information Security Management Standard
Ye batata hai company ko data security kaise manage karni chahiye.
Main points:-
Risk management
Access control
Security policies
Incident management
Audit logs

Example:-
Company ko ensure karna hai ki sirf authorized user hi database access kare.

2️⃣ SOC 2:-
SOC 2 = Service Organization Control
Ye mostly cloud companies ke liye hota hai.
Isme 5 trust principles hote hain:-
1️⃣ Security
2️⃣ Availability
3️⃣ Processing integrity
4️⃣ Confidentiality
5️⃣ Privacy

Example:-
Customer data unauthorized user ko visible nahi hona chahiye.

3️⃣ GDPR:-
GDPR = General Data Protection Regulation
Ye Europe ka data protection law hai.
Main rule
User ka personal data protect karna zaroori hai.
Example:-
User agar bole mera data delete karo
company ko delete karna padega

4️⃣ HIPAA:-
HIPAA = Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act
Ye medical data protection law hai.
Hospitals aur healthcare companies ko follow karna padta hai.
Example:-
Patient ka medical record unauthorized user nahi dekh sakta.

5️⃣ Sarbanes‑Oxley Act (SOX):-
SOX = Financial security law
Ye companies ke financial data ko protect karta hai.
Example:-
Finance employee ko financial database ka access control hona chahiye.
