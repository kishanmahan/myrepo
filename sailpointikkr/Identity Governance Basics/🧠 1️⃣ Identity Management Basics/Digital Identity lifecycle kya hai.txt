()Digital Identity lifecycle kya hai?
--1️⃣ Joiner (Identity Creation):-
Jab naya employee join karta hai company me.
SailPoint me Identity object create hota hai, aur uske liye required accounts & access provision hote hain.
Example:-
Identity rahul = new Identity();
rahul.setName("Rahul Kumar");
rahul.setEmail("rahul@company.com");
rahul.setDepartment("Finance");
rahul.setRole("Finance Manager");
rahul.setStatus("Active");

// Provision access
provisionAccess(rahul);
--------------------------
()2️⃣ Mover (Identity Update / Transfer)
Jab employee ka role, department, ya location change hota hai.
SailPoint me Identity update hoti hai, aur access adjust hota hai.

Example:-
// Rahul ka department change
rahul.setDepartment("IT");
rahul.setRole("IT Manager");

// Re-evaluate access based on new role
updateAccess(rahul);
----------------------
()3️⃣ Leaver (Identity Deactivation / Removal):-
Jab employee company chhod deta hai.
SailPoint me Identity deactivate ya delete hoti hai, aur saare access revoke hote hain.
Example:-
rahul.setStatus("Inactive");

// Revoke all access
deprovisionAccess(rahul);
