📘 ROLE MANAGEMENT – COMPLETE CHAPTERS:-
🟢 CHAPTER 1: Role Management Overview:-
Role kya hota hai?
👉 Role = Entitlements ka logical group.

Instead of:-
10 group manually assign karna

We assign:-
1 role

🟢 CHAPTER 2: RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)
RBAC concept:-
User → Role → Entitlements → Application
Benefits:-
Simplified provisioning
Better governance
Easy certification
Reduced manual errors

🟢 CHAPTER 3: Types of Roles
In SailPoint IdentityIQ mainly:

1️⃣ Business Role
Business function based
Example: HR Executive

2️⃣ IT Role
Technical entitlement bundle
Example: AD + SAP access

3️⃣ Application Role
Single app specific role
