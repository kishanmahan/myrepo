📘 CERTIFICATION – ALL METHODS & TYPES:-
🟢 1️⃣ Manager Certification
Kya hota hai?
Manager apne team members ka access review karta hai.
Review karta hai:-
Accounts
Entitlements
Roles

Actions:-
✔ Approve
❌ Revoke
💬 Add comment

Use case:-
SOX / Internal audit compliance

🟢 2️⃣ Application Owner Certification
Kya hota hai?
Application owner review karta hai:-
Kaun users us application me access rakhte hain

Owner confirm karta hai:-
Business justified hai ya nahi

🟢 3️⃣ Role Certification
Review hota hai:-
Role members
Role ke entitlements

Check:-
Role sahi bana hai?
Koi extra entitlement to nahi?

🟢 4️⃣ Entitlement (Managed Attribute) Certification
Review hota hai:-
Specific group / privilege
High-risk entitlements

Example:-
Domain Admin group review.

🟢 5️⃣ Account Certification
Review hota hai:-
Application accounts
Especially orphan accounts

Check:-
Account active hona chahiye?
Disable karna hai?

🟢 6️⃣ Identity Certification
Review hota hai:-
Entire identity access
All linked accounts
All roles
Risk score
Most comprehensive review type 🔥

🟢 7️⃣ Targeted Certification
Specific criteria based:-
Department = Finance
Location = US
High-risk users only
Filter-based certification.

🟢 8️⃣ Event-Based Certification
Trigger hota hai:-
Joiner
Mover
Policy violation
High-risk detection
Real-time governance.

🟢 9️⃣ Policy Violation Certification
If SoD violation detect hota hai:-
→ Certification generate hoti hai
→ Reviewer decide karega approve ya revoke

🟢 🔟 Risk-Based Certification
High risk identity →
Automatic certification trigger
Risk score based review.

🟢 11️⃣ Periodic Certification
Schedule based:-
Monthly
Quarterly
Yearly
Compliance regulation ke liye.

🟢 12️⃣ Continuous Certification
Rolling basis pe:-
New access granted
Immediately review required
Advanced governance model.

🟢 13️⃣ Ad-Hoc Certification
Manual create ki jati hai:-
Audit demand
Investigation
Emergency review

🟢 14️⃣ Hierarchical Certification
Multi-level review:-
Manager →
Application owner →
Security team

🟢 15️⃣ Delegated Certification
If reviewer unavailable:-
Delegate to another user
Temporary reassignment

🟢 16️⃣ Revocation-Based Certification
If revoke select kare:-
→ Deprovision trigger
→ Access remove
→ Audit log store

🟢 17️⃣ Expiry-Based Review
Temporary access expire hone se pehle:-
→ Review trigger
→ Extend ya revoke

🟢 18️⃣ Campaign-Based Certification
Large organization wide campaign:-
All employees
All apps
Defined timeframe
Used in audits.

🟢 19️⃣ Automated Decision Certification
Based on rule:-
Low risk → Auto approve
High risk → Manual review

🟢 20️⃣ Exception Certification
If violation accepted:-
Mitigation record store
Justification mandatory
Audit safe.

🟢 Certification Actions
During review reviewer kar sakta hai:-
✔ Approve
❌ Revoke
↩ Reassign
⏳ Delegate
💬 Comment

🟢 Certification Workflow
Flow:-
Campaign create →
Reviewer assign →
Access review →
Decision →
Provisioning trigger (if revoke) →
Audit store

🟢 Certification Evidence
Stored:-
Who reviewed
When reviewed
What decision
Justification comment
Provisioning status
Audit ke liye critical 🔥

🟢 Certification Reports
Generate kar sakte ho:-
Completed campaigns
Open reviews
Revoked access
High-risk identities
